## 示例：都市重生小说压缩 / Example: Urban Reborn Novel Compression

### 中文

#### 输入内容

**小说名**：重生之最强赘婿
**字数**：80章 / 约30万字
**核心剧情**：林凡，入赘三年的上门女婿，被老婆和丈母娘看不起，其实是隐藏的商业大佬，重生回来复仇。

#### 输出结果

**3秒钩子**
> "入赘三年被骂废物，丈母娘让他当众下跪。可他只说了一句话，整个公司的人都傻了——'我叫林凡，林氏集团唯一的继承人。'"

**30秒剧情入口**
> 入赘三年，林凡被老婆和丈母娘骂了三年废物。直到那天拍卖会，他随手拍下一件百万古董……原来他不是废物，而是传说中那个掌控半个城市商业的神秘投资人。

**1分钟高能版**

| 时间 | 节奏点 | 内容 |
|------|--------|------|
| 0-15秒 | 第一个爽点 | 丈母娘当众羞辱林凡，让他跪下 |
| 15-30秒 | 第二个爽点 | 林凡站起来，全场安静 |
| 30-45秒 | 第三个爽点 | 林凡掏出股权证，丈母娘当场傻眼 |
| 45-60秒 | 第四个爽点 | 宣布收购丈母娘家公司，全家下跪求饶 |

**3分钟高能版**

0-60秒（铺垫）：
- 林凡入赘三年，被骂废物
- 第一次小反杀：林凡一句话让丈母娘吃瘪
- 第二次小爽点：神秘大佬出现，对林凡毕恭毕敬

60-150秒（高潮）：
- 身份正式揭开：林凡是林氏集团继承人
- 第一波反杀：开除骂他的主管
- 第二波反杀：冻结丈母娘家的公司账户
- 名场面：股权证亮出，全场傻眼

150-180秒（结局）：
- 终极反杀：成功收购丈母娘家公司
- 跪舔时刻：丈母娘全家来公司门口下跪
- 情感收尾：林凡看着曾经羞辱他的人，选择复仇但不失风度

**结局速通版**
> 三个核心反转：1.入赘废物是豪门继承人 2.收购羞辱他的公司 3.仇家全部下跪求饶

**角色关系图**
```
林凡（隐藏继承人）←羞辱/反杀→ 丈母娘（嚣张恶人）
     ↓                    ↓
     └──→ 老婆（摇摆不定）←──→ 商业对手
```

**高能片段索引**
| 片段 | 章节 | 场景描述 |
|------|------|----------|
| 1 | 第5章 | 丈母娘当众羞辱林凡 |
| 2 | 第15章 | 神秘大佬对林凡毕恭恭敬 |
| 3 | 第30章 | 身份正式揭开 |
| 4 | 第50章 | 收购丈母娘家公司 |
| 5 | 第65章 | 仇家下跪道歉 |

#### 解说稿示例（1分钟版）

> 你敢信吗？这个被丈母娘骂了三年的废物赘婿，竟然是林氏集团的唯一继承人！
>
> 那天拍卖会，他随手举牌买下一件百万古董。所有人都嘲笑他穷疯了，可当股权证明书亮出来的那一刻——丈母娘当场腿软了。
>
> 入赘三年，他受尽屈辱。今天，他让所有羞辱他的人，一分不差地还回来！

### English

#### Input

**Novel title**: Rebirth: The Strongest Live-in Son-in-Law
**Length**: 80 chapters / ~300,000 words
**Core plot**: Lin Fan, a live-in son-in-law for three years, looked down on by his wife and mother-in-law, is actually a hidden business tycoon who has been reborn to take revenge.

#### Output

**3-Second Hook**
> "For three years he was called trash; his mother-in-law made him kneel in public. But he said just one sentence, and everyone in the company went numb—'My name is Lin Fan, the sole heir of the Lin Group.'"

**30-Second Intro**
> For three years, Lin Fan was called trash by his wife and mother-in-law. Until that auction, when he casually bought a million-dollar antique... Turns out he was no trash, but the mysterious investor who controlled half the city's business.

**1-Minute Highlight Version**

| Time | Beat | Content |
|------|------|---------|
| 0-15s | Highlight 1 | Mother-in-law publicly humiliates Lin Fan, makes him kneel |
| 15-30s | Highlight 2 | Lin Fan stands up, the whole room falls silent |
| 30-45s | Highlight 3 | Lin Fan pulls out the equity certificate; mother-in-law is stunned |
| 45-60s | Highlight 4 | Announces acquisition of mother-in-law's company; whole family kneels and begs |

**3-Minute Highlight Version**

0-60s (build-up):
- Lin Fan has been a live-in son-in-law for three years, called trash
- First small comeback: Lin silences the mother-in-law with one sentence
- Second small highlight: A mysterious big shot appears, deeply respectful to Lin

60-150s (climax):
- Identity officially revealed: Lin is the heir of the Lin Group
- First wave of revenge: Fires the manager who insulted him
- Second wave: Freezes mother-in-law's company account
- Iconic scene: Equity certificate revealed; the whole room is stunned

150-180s (ending):
- Ultimate comeback: Successfully acquires mother-in-law's company
- Groveling moment: Mother-in-law's whole family kneels at the company gate
- Emotional close: Lin looks at those who once humiliated him; chooses revenge with dignity

**Ending Rush Version**
> Three core reversals: 1. The "trash" son-in-law is a hidden heir 2. Acquires the company that humiliated him 3. All enemies kneel and beg

**Character Relationship Map**
```
Lin Fan (hidden heir) ←humiliation/comeback→ Mother-in-law (arrogant villain)
     ↓                                ↓
     └──→ Wife (wavering) ←──→ Business rival
```

**High-Point Segment Index**

| 2 | Chapter 15 | Mysterious big shot deeply respectful to Lin |

#### Narration Script Sample (1-min version)

> Can you believe it? The trash son-in-law that his mother-in-law insulted for three years is actually the sole heir of the Lin Group!
>
> At the auction that day, he casually bid on a million-dollar antique. Everyone laughed at him for being broke and crazy, but the moment the equity certificate came out—the mother-in-law's legs went weak.
>
> Three years of being a live-in son-in-law, three years of humiliation. Today, he makes everyone who ever insulted him pay back every bit of it!
